# Railgun feedback plan

Planned for a later combat-feedback pass:

- Distinct hit-confirmation sound for a successful railgun hit.
- Stronger confirmation for a kill.
- Small temporary medal icons in the upper-left corner.
- Repeated medals stack into a compact counter instead of covering the playfield.
- End-of-match summary shows earned medals and totals.
- Candidate medals: direct hit, carrier stop, long-range hit, multi-hit streak, and rail kill streak.
- Feedback must be derived from combat events rather than inferred from visual effects.
